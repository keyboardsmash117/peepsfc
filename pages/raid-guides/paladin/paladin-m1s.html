🛡️🐱 Paladin vs Black Cat (Savage) 🐱🛡️

Master precise positioning, cooldown timing, and tank swaps against the agile Black Cat! 🐱⚡

"Holy protection guides swift strikes" ⚔️✨

⚔️ Paladin Phase Timeline

🐱 Phase 1: Opening (0:00 - 2:30)

🛡️ Tank Responsibilities:
  • Pull positioning: Center Black Cat, face away from party
  • Initial combo: Start with Fight or Flight opener
  • First tank buster: Use Sentinel for "Savage Pounce"
  • Claw patterns: Move precisely with claw telegraph directions
⚔️ Rotation Notes:
  • Maintain Goring Blade DoT throughout
  • Use Intervene to quickly reposition after dodges
  • Keep Fight or Flight on cooldown

⚠️ Critical: Never let Black Cat face the party during Savage Pounce!

⚡ Phase 2: Lightning Combos (2:30 - 5:00)

🛡️ Enhanced Mechanics:
  • Lightning Claw Combo: Requires Hallowed Ground
  • Tank swap timing: After 3rd Lightning Claw stack
  • Positioning: Coordinate with co-tank for smooth transitions
  • Party protection: Use Divine Veil for raid damage
💫 Cooldown Priority:
  1. Hallowed Ground - Lightning Claw Combo (1st use)
  2. Sentinel + Rampart - Heavy tank buster
  3. Divine Veil - Before AoE phases

💡 Pro Tip: Pre-position for tank swaps during Lightning Claw cast!

🔥 Final Phase: Enrage Rush (5:00 - 6:30)

⚔️ Maximum DPS Phase:
  • Rotation priority: All DPS cooldowns available
  • Final tank buster: Sentinel + Rampart combo
  • Emergency tools: Clemency if healers stressed
  • Positioning: Perfect uptime required for DPS check
🛡️ Emergency Situations:
  • Low HP: Use Clemency between GCDs
  • Co-tank down: Cover vulnerable party members
  • Enrage timer: Focus pure DPS rotation

🚨 Enrage at 6:30 - Every GCD matters in final phase!

🎯 Paladin-Specific Mechanics

🔄 Tank Swap Execution

  1. Watch for 3rd Lightning Claw debuff stack
  2. Provoke during boss cast bar (not after)
  3. Use Intervene to quickly move to MT position
  4. Begin mitigation rotation immediately
  5. Off-tank: Shirk to new MT after swap

🛡️ Cooldown Rotation

  • Savage Pounce 1: Sentinel
  • Lightning Combo: Hallowed Ground
  • Savage Pounce 2: Rampart + Bulwark
  • Final Buster: Sentinel + Rampart
  • Emergency: Clemency ready

❌ Common Paladin Mistakes in M1S

🚫 Positioning Errors

Facing Black Cat toward party during tank busters - always keep boss facing away!

⏰ Cooldown Waste

Using Hallowed Ground too early or saving it for "emergencies" - use it on schedule!

🔄 Swap Delays

Late tank swaps causing stack overflow - swap during cast, not after!
