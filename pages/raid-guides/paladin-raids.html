🛡️ GENERAL PALADIN TIPS 🛡️

⚔️ ROTATION BASICS

  • Maintain Fight or Flight buff
  • Use Requiescat for magic phase
  • Keep Goring Blade DoT up
  • Weave oGCDs between combos
  • Use Intervene for gap closing

🛡️ DEFENSIVE STRATEGY

  • Hallowed Ground for tank busters
  • Sentinel for heavy damage
  • Divine Veil for party shields
  • Cover to protect others
  • Clemency for emergency heals

💕 Master these fundamentals before tackling specific fights! 💕
