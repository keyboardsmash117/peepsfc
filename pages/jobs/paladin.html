🛡️ Quick Paladin Reference 🛡️

⚔️ Key Abilities

  • Fight or Flight: Main damage buff
  • Requiescat: Magic phase starter
  • Hallowed Ground: Immunity cooldown
  • Goring Blade: DoT combo finisher

🛡️ Role in Party

  • Main Tank: Lead enemy positioning
  • Party Support: Shields and healing
  • Emergency Healer: Clemency backup
  • Damage Dealer: High personal DPS
