📜✨ Free Company Rules ✨📜

🌸💖 Guidelines for a harmonious community in Eorzea 💖🌸

🌸💖 Welcome to Peeps FC! 💖🌸

These guidelines help us maintain a welcoming, respectful, and fun community for all adventurers! ✨🌟

"💫 The bonds we forge light the way forward! 💫" - Our FC Motto 🌸

🌟 1. General Conduct & Respect

  • Treat all FC members, allies, and players with respect and kindness
  • No harassment, discrimination, or toxic behavior of any kind
  • Keep discussions civil and constructive
  • Respect different playstyles, schedules, and skill levels
  • No drama or personal conflicts in FC chat or Discord
  • Use appropriate language - avoid excessive profanity
  • No political or controversial topics in FC spaces

💬 2. Communication Guidelines

  • Join our Discord server for better communication and events
  • Use appropriate channels for different topics (general, raid planning, etc.)
  • No spam or excessive caps lock in chat
  • Keep FC chat friendly and welcoming to new members
  • Ask questions freely - we're here to help each other learn
  • Use @mentions responsibly and avoid unnecessary pings

⚔️ 3. Activity & Participation

  • No minimum activity requirement, but communicate long absences
  • Inactive members (90+ days without notice) may be removed to make space
  • Participate in FC events when possible - it builds community!
  • Help newer members with content when you can
  • Contribute to FC buffs when able (not required)
  • Let officers know if you plan extended breaks

🏠 4. FC Housing & Company Chest

  • FC house is for all members to enjoy - treat it with respect
  • Don't move or remove furniture without permission
  • Company chest items are shared resources - take only what you need
  • Contribute materials and gil when you can to support the FC
  • Higher rank members have responsibility to maintain chest organization
  • No hoarding expensive items from the chest

🎯 5. Events & Group Content

  • Sign up for events in advance when possible
  • Notify organizers if you can't attend after signing up
  • Come prepared with appropriate gear, food, and pots for raids
  • Be patient during learning parties - everyone was new once
  • Follow the raid leader's instructions during content
  • Share loot fairly and considerately
  • No rage quitting during group content

⭐ 6. Ranks & Responsibilities

  • Ranks are earned through activity, helpfulness, and positive attitude
  • Higher ranks come with greater responsibilities to help the FC
  • Officers are here to help - don't hesitate to reach out
  • Promotions are based on contribution, not time served
  • Use rank permissions responsibly
  • Report issues to officers rather than handling conflicts yourself

⚠️ 7. Rule Violations & Consequences

  • Minor violations: Verbal warning and guidance
  • Repeated offenses: Temporary removal from FC chat/events
  • Serious violations: Immediate removal from FC
  • Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, or malicious behavior
  • Appeals can be made to FC leadership
  • Officers will investigate fairly before taking action

💖 Remember

These rules exist to create a welcoming, fun environment for everyone. When in doubt, choose kindness. We're all here to enjoy Final Fantasy XIV together!

"The bonds we forge light the way forward." - Peeps FC Motto
