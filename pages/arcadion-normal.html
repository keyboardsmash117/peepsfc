🏟️ The Arcadion (Normal)

Dawntrail's Level 100 Normal Raid Series

✨ AAC Light-heavyweight Tier ✨

The first tier of Arcadion raids, featuring intense 8-player battles in the arena! 🏟️

"Step into the ring and prove your worth!" 💪✨

🥊 AAC Light-heavyweight M1

Boss: Black Cat

Face the agile Black Cat in this opening bout! Watch out for rapid strikes and acrobatic attacks. 🐱⚡

💡 FC Tip: Stay mobile and coordinate with your team for positioning!

🍯 AAC Light-heavyweight M2

Boss: Honey B Lovely

Sweet but deadly! Honey B Lovely brings bee-themed mechanics and area control challenges. 🐝💕

💡 FC Tip: Mind the honey patches and coordinate movement patterns!

💣 AAC Light-heavyweight M3

Boss: Brute Bomber

Explosive encounters await! Brute Bomber tests your reflexes with bomb mechanics and heavy hits. 💥🔥

💡 FC Tip: Timing is everything - watch those bomb timers carefully!

🤖 AAC Light-heavyweight M4

Boss: Eutrope

The tier finale! Face the mechanical marvel Eutrope in an epic showdown with complex mechanics. ⚙️✨

💡 FC Tip: Master the rotation phases and stay calm under pressure!
